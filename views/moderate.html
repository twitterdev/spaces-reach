Pet explorer

Make your pet related conversations more a-meow-zing. This app lets you manage replies that are not pet related, based on Tweet annotations (Twitter's artificial intelligence to detect topic and entities of a Tweet).

How to make Pet explorer work for you:

  1. Ask your friends on Twitter to reply to your Tweet with a story, fact, or favorite pet name. There needs to be at least pet mentioned in their Tweet.
  2. Get the ID of your Tweet (on Twitter, click on
    , then paste it in the field below.
  3. If the Tweet has replies, it will show you whether it contains pet-related replies or not, based on Twitter's understanding of that reply.
  4. Hide and unhide replies following the app's suggestion.
